It All Started Peacefully...

One fine morning, I decided to fix a simple computer issue. “Easy job,” I said. I even made tea. Big mistake.

Then Chaos Hit

Monkey Business

While I was installing drivers, a monkey (yes, a real monkey) snuck through the window and ran off with my mouse. I chased it through three compounds barefoot.

The Blue Screen of Doom

After recovering my mouse (and my breath), I plugged everything back in—only to be greeted by a glorious blue screen. I think even the monkey laughed.

What I Learned

1. Always close your window. 2. Monkeys love gadgets. 3. Never say "easy job" out loud. It jinxes everything.
